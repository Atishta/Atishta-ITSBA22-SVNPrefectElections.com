SVN PREFECT E-VOTING

Welcome to SVN's first prefect e-voting initiative aimed at
providing a fair election for the head and class prefects. Be sure
to navigate around our website to familarize yourself with the
voting process, cast your vote, and even track the polls live.
Also, do not hesitate to email us if you have any concerns.

HOW TO VOTE

------

In order to vote in this election, you first have to visit the voting webpage. There, you will have access to two voting forms/ e-ballots- one for the head prefect elections and the other for the class prefect elections. Before actually voting you will be mandated to register for each elections. After registering, you will be presented with the candidates and for each category, you will be required to vote for one male and one female. Everyone has to vote for the head prefects but with regards to class prefect elections, you are only required to vote for the candidates from your class. Do not vote for candidates from other classes.
